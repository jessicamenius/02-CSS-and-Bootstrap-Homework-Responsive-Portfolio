Why am I taking this course?

"I choose a lazy person to do a hard job. Because a lazy person will find an easy way to do it." - Bill Gates

This quote has always inspired me. I'm not "lazy". However, I have always taken a look at what is being done and tried to figure out a way to make it easier and more efficient. Why do something in 12 steps when you can do it in 6.

This is why I want to learn to code. I want to be able to use ways to automate or make work much easier for myself. I also want to learn to code so I can speak intelligently with those who are doing the coding for projects I work on at work.
